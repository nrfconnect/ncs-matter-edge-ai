If you want to commission the |matter_name| device to control another Matter accessory, such as smart bulb, through a Thread network, you need a Matter Controller with a `Thread Border Router <Thread tools_#ug-thread-tools-tbr>`_ (TBR) functionality on that network.
You can use `chip-tool`_ or a commercial Matter ecosystem controller.
A commercial home hub that supports Matter and includes TBR capability (for example, Apple HomePod 2nd gen. or Apple HomePod mini, Google Home Speaker, or Amazon Echo with Thread) can replace both a standalone OTBR and CHIP Tool.
You can also control it using a Matter controller `configured on PC or smartphone <Testing Matter in the NCS_>`_.
This requires additional hardware depending on the setup you choose.
