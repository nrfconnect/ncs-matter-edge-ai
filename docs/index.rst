|addon| for the |NCS|
#####################

The |addon| for the |NCS| combines `Matter`_ smart-home protocol support with Edge AI capabilities.
It enables development of Matter accessories that use on-device inference.

The add-on builds on the `Edge AI add-on`_ (version |edge-ai_version|) and connects it with Matter applications in the |NCS|.
In combination with the |NCS|, the |addon| allows for development of Matter Edge AI applications.

.. note::
   The |addon| is provided as an `experimental feature <Software maturity levels_>`_.

For additional context on other solutions, refer to the following resources:

* `Matter overview`_ — Introduction to the Matter protocol and its use in the |NCS|.
* `Matter architecture`_ — How Matter accessories, controllers, and networks fit together.
* `Edge AI add-on`_ — nRF Edge AI library, tooling, and general Edge AI samples (version |edge-ai_version|).
* `Edge AI add-on repository`_ — Source code for the Edge AI add-on.
* `Nordic Edge AI Lab`_ — Web-based environment for training and exporting Edge AI models used in Matter Edge AI applications.
